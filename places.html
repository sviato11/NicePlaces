Spain, Madrid, Park Retiro

The artificial lake in Retiro Park was brought in 1630 for the Spanish royal family to relax. One of its unique features is the Crystal Palace (Palacio de Cristal), which was originally built for a Philippines plant exhibition and has become a symbol of the blend of nature and art.

United Kingdom, London, Elizabeth Tower

Although people often refer to the entire tower as "Big Ben," the name actually belongs to the bell itself, which weighs 13.5 tons and is the largest bell in the United Kingdom.

Italy, Rome, Colosseum

The Colosseum in Rome could hold up to 50,000 spectators and was used for various events in ancient times, including gladiator fights, mock naval battles (the arena was flooded), and public executions.
